How it works

The SimpleTen AI Modernization Plan

A clear, low-risk path from "we should probably use AI" to workflows running quietly in the background.

Discover

We start by understanding how your business actually works. We review your tools, interview your team, identify repetitive tasks, and find where AI and automation can create the most immediate value.

You leave with a clear roadmap of what to automate first, what to avoid, and where the biggest return is hiding.

Design

We design the workflows, agents, prompts, triggers, and human approval points before anything goes live. This keeps the system practical, safe, and aligned with your team's real process.

You get a simple plan for how each workflow should function, who it supports, and what success looks like.

Deploy

We build, test, and launch the workflows inside your current business environment. Your team gets support, documentation, and refinement so the systems keep working as your business evolves.

The goal is simple: useful AI that saves time, improves follow-up, and quietly keeps the business moving.
